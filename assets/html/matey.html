Try out your RML mappings without installing anything, in your browser. You can add your input data on the left (yellow box), write your mapping rules in the middle (green box) and get the generated RDF (purple box). Some examples are available as well to get you started quickly. You can load those below by selecting one in the dropdown and click on the 'Load Example' button. Once loaded, you can edit them as you wish.

This playground demonstrates the following RML specifications: {{{RMLspecifications}}}. The RML mapping engine {{{RMLengine}}} generates RDF data.
